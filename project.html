Rock Paper Scissors - Hand Recognition

Instructions

Use your hand gestures to play Rock, Paper, Scissors against the COMPUTER!

  • Rock: Make a fist.
  • Paper: Open your hand flat.
  • Scissors: Extend your index and middle fingers.
